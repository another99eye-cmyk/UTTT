4. Gameplay Requirements (Ultimate Tic Tac Toe)
4.1 Board System

Big Board: 3×3 grid of small boards

Small Board: 3×3 grid of cells

4.2 Rules Enforcement

Forced move logic based on previous move

Detect:

Small board win

Small board draw

Big board win

Big board draw

Lock completed small boards

Visual indication of active board(s)

4.3 Player System

Player X / Player O

Turn tracking

Move validation

Illegal move prevention

5. Input System (Mobile-Oriented)

Touch-based input abstraction

Rect-based hit detection

Large tap targets

No keyboard dependency

Gesture-safe interactions

6. AI System Requirements
Easy AI

Random valid moves only

Normal AI

Heuristic evaluation

Center preference

Blocking immediate wins

Favor winning small boards

Hard AI

Minimax with alpha-beta pruning

Depth-limited search

Cached board evaluations

Hard time or depth cutoff to prevent frame drops
